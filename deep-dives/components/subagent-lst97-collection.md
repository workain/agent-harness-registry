A full-stack-focused subagent collection that started as personal use and gained broader traction.

You want a full-stack-web-dev-specific roster rather than a broad multi-domain collection.

Copy relevant subagent files into `.claude/agents/`.

Narrower/more focused than VoltAgent's 10-category spread — pick this if full-stack web dev is specifically your use case.
