# Aider

**Registry entry:** `data/engines/aider.yaml`

## What it is

Terminal-based AI pair-programming tool: builds a "repo map" of the whole codebase to give the
model context-aware structure, applies edits as git commits with generated messages, and supports
100+ languages. Works with most major model providers, including local models.
